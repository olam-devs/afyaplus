Who We Are

Healthcare Made Accessible for Tanzania

AfyaPlus was founded with a singular mission: to remove barriers between Tanzanians and quality healthcare. We believe that where you live, your income, or the distance to the nearest hospital should never determine the quality of care you receive.

Through the power of WhatsApp — a platform already in every Tanzanian's pocket — we connect patients with licensed, professional doctors instantly, affordably, and securely.

  • Board-certified general practitioners and specialists
  • Bilingual service in Swahili and English
  • Available nationwide — no travel required
  • Secure, private, and confidential consultations
