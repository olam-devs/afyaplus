Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the questions we hear most often from patients and businesses.

How do I access AfyaPlus services?
All AfyaPlus services are accessed through WhatsApp. Simply send a message to our number, select your preferred language (Swahili or English), choose your service, and follow the simple steps. No app download is required.
Are the doctors on AfyaPlus properly licensed?
Yes. Every doctor and health professional working with AfyaPlus is fully licensed and certified by the relevant Tanzanian medical authorities. We conduct thorough vetting of credentials before any professional joins our platform.
Is my health information kept private and confidential?
Absolutely. Your health information is strictly confidential. We adhere to medical privacy standards and do not share your personal health data with any third parties without your explicit consent.
What payment methods do you accept?
AfyaPlus accepts all major mobile money payments including M-Pesa, Airtel Money, and Tigo Pesa, as well as card payments. You will receive a payment link after selecting your service on WhatsApp.
Can AfyaPlus services be used outside Dar es Salaam?
Yes. Telehealth services (GP and specialist consultations, pharmacy advisory) are available nationwide across Tanzania — all you need is an internet or data connection. Home doctor visits are currently available in major urban centres; contact us for availability in your area.
Is AfyaPlus suitable for children and elderly patients?
Yes. AfyaPlus serves patients of all ages. We have paediatric specialists for children's health concerns, and our home doctor service is particularly popular for elderly patients who find travel difficult. Family members can also consult on behalf of a loved one.

Medical Emergency?

AfyaPlus is not an emergency service. If you or someone around you is experiencing a life-threatening emergency, please call Tanzania Emergency Services: 112 or go directly to your nearest hospital emergency department immediately.
